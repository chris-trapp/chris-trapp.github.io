How I think about the job

Three things I believe make a great product manager.

I
Listen before you define
Before I define anything, I ask a lot of questions. Of the team, the customers, the backlog. The first 30 days on any product are intelligence-gathering. Everything after is better for it.
II
Connect the data to the story
Data without context gets ignored. I pair the analysis with a clear business narrative so stakeholders understand what they're looking at and what to do about it.
III
Own the outcome, not the output
I define KPIs before we launch, monitor adoption after, and stay accountable to the business result. The delivery date matters. What it delivers matters more.
